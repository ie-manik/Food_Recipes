Chocolate Lava Cake

Prep: 10 min  |  Cook: 12 min  |  Serves: 2  |  Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 100g dark chocolate (chopped)
  • 100g unsalted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 80g powdered sugar
  • 40g all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Cocoa powder (for dusting)
  • Vanilla ice cream (for serving)
← Back to All Recipes

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Preheat your oven to 220°C (425°F). Grease two individual ramekins generously with butter, then dust lightly with cocoa powder, shaking out any excess. This ensures the cakes release cleanly.

2

Place the chopped dark chocolate and butter together in a heatproof bowl. Melt them over a double boiler (a pot of simmering water) or in the microwave in 30-second bursts, stirring between each, until completely smooth. Let it cool slightly.

3

In a separate bowl, whisk together the whole eggs, egg yolks, and powdered sugar until the mixture turns pale and slightly thickened, about 2 minutes.

4

Pour the cooled chocolate mixture into the egg mixture and add the vanilla extract and a pinch of salt. Gently fold together until combined.

5

Sift in the all-purpose flour and fold gently with a spatula until just incorporated. Do not overmix. Divide the batter evenly between the two prepared ramekins.

6

Bake in the preheated oven for exactly 10-12 minutes. The edges should be set and firm but the centre should still wobble slightly when shaken. Run a knife around the edge, invert onto a plate, and serve immediately with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
