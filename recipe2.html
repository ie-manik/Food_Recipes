Butter Chicken

Prep: 20 min  |  Cook: 30 min  |  Serves: 4  |  Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (boneless, cubed)
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 large onion (chopped)
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 inch ginger (grated)
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves
← Back to All Recipes

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Marinate the chicken cubes with 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1/2 tsp red chilli powder, salt, and 2 tbsp plain yogurt. Mix well and set aside for at least 15 minutes.

2

Heat 1 tbsp butter in a pan over high heat. Add the marinated chicken pieces and cook for 5-6 minutes until lightly browned and cooked through. Remove and set aside.

3

In the same pan, add remaining butter. Sauté the chopped onion until golden brown (about 5 minutes). Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, cook for another 2 minutes.

4

Add the cumin powder, coriander powder, remaining red chilli powder, and garam masala. Stir and cook the spices for 1 minute until fragrant.

5

Pour in the tomato puree and cook on medium heat for 8-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens and the oil separates at the edges.

6

Reduce heat to low. Pour in the heavy cream and stir well. Add the cooked chicken back to the pan. Simmer for 5 minutes. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.
